# AGENTS.MD - AI Context

## Project Profile
This repository is a technical learning journal of a Senior Engineer. Its goal is to bridge the gap between traditional engineering and AI-assisted development.

## Writing Guidelines
When assisting me in drafting or editing files:
1. **Value Duality:** Ensure content provides value both to someone who doesn't know what a 'Prompt' is and to someone who designs distributed systems.
2. **Focus on the "Why":** Don't just document the command; document the reasoning behind the technology.
3. **Validation:** If you suggest code, it must be modular and follow SOLID/Clean Code principles.

## Markdown Standards
- Hierarchical titles (H1 for title, H2 sections, H3 subsections).
- Use of Callouts (Note, Warning, Tip) to highlight key information.
- Mermaid.js diagrams whenever possible to explain flows.

## What we DO NOT want
- Generic AI-generated content without "soul" or human validation.
- Excessively long explanations of basic concepts that can be summarized in a list.
